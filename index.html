What are natural remedies for a sore throat?+
Raw honey mixed in warm water (documented since 8,000 BCE) coats and soothes the throat with antimicrobial properties. Black seed oil (Nigella sativa) has been used since ancient Egypt for respiratory and throat conditions. Ginger tea with lemon reduces inflammation. Remedy's AI can walk you through preparation and dosage for each.
What helps with flu symptoms at home?+
Traditional remedies for flu include honey and black seed in warm water for immune support, ginger tea for nausea and inflammation, turmeric milk (golden milk) for immune response, and garlic for its documented antimicrobial properties. Stay hydrated and rest. If you develop a high fever, chest pain, or breathing difficulty, seek medical care immediately.
Are there natural remedies for high blood pressure?+
Garlic (used since 5,000 BCE in Egyptian and Greek medicine) has documented blood pressure-lowering effects. Olive oil, black seed, and reducing sodium intake are also historically supported approaches. However, high blood pressure requires medical monitoring — Remedy recommends consulting a doctor, which you can do affordably using our credit system from $19.99.
What natural remedies help with anxiety?+
Chamomile tea (used since 2,000 BCE in Egyptian and Greek medicine) is the most widely documented natural anxiety remedy and is supported by multiple clinical studies. Honey has calming properties, and regular use of ginger supports the gut-brain connection. For persistent anxiety, a licensed professional can help — book a consultation from $19.99.

What are the proven benefits of black seed (Nigella sativa)?+
Black seed has been used medicinally since 3,300 BCE and is documented in ancient Egyptian, Greek, Persian, and Arabian medicine. Modern peer-reviewed research supports its use for immune function, respiratory health, anti-inflammatory effects, and mild blood pressure support. Its active compound, thymoquinone, has been studied extensively. Remedy's natural remedies mode covers full preparation guidance.
