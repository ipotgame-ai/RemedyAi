What are natural remedies for a sore throat?+
Raw honey in warm water (documented since 8,000 BCE) coats and soothes the throat. Black seed oil has been used since ancient Egypt for respiratory conditions. Ginger tea with lemon reduces inflammation. Ask Remedy's AI for exact preparation and dosage.
What helps with flu symptoms at home?+
Traditional flu remedies include honey and black seed in warm water for immune support, ginger tea for nausea and inflammation, turmeric milk, and garlic for its antimicrobial properties. If symptoms include chest pain, difficulty breathing or high fever in children, seek emergency care immediately.
Are there natural remedies for high blood pressure?+
Garlic (documented since 5,000 BCE), olive oil, black seed, and reducing sodium are historically supported approaches. High blood pressure requires medical monitoring — Remedy always recommends seeing a doctor, which you can do affordably from $35 using our credit system.

What are the benefits of black seed (Nigella sativa)?+
Black seed has been documented since 3,300 BCE in Egyptian, Greek, Persian and Arabian medicine. Modern research supports its use for immune function, respiratory health, and anti-inflammatory effects. Its active compound thymoquinone has been extensively studied. Remedy's natural remedies mode provides full preparation guidance.

Remedy provides general wellness information — not diagnosis or treatment. Every response includes safety notes, and Remedy automatically detects emergency symptoms (chest pain, stroke signs, severe breathing difficulty) and immediately directs you to call 911. It is not a substitute for professional medical care.

Fenugreek (used since 4,000 BCE) has documented blood-sugar-lowering effects. Turmeric's curcumin supports insulin sensitivity. Cinnamon and bitter melon are also historically used. Remedy provides full guidance, and always recommends monitoring with a physician — book one from $35.
